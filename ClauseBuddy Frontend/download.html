ClauseBuddy

Stop skimming, start knowing. Our AI instantly highlights data risks, hidden costs, and privacy loopholes in any legal agreement.

Download Package

Supports Chrome, Edge, and Brave browsers.

Setup 01

Extract Source

Locate clausebuddy.zip in your downloads. Extract it to a permanent folder on your machine.

Setup 02

Dev Mode

Navigate to chrome://extensions and toggle the Developer Mode switch to ON.

Setup 03

Load Extension

Click Load Unpacked and select the inner extension folder from your extracted files.

Setup 04

Toolbar Pin

Open the puzzle icon in your browser and Pin Clause Buddy to start your first legal analysis.
